ABOUT US

Declared open in the year 2001 by the legendary math educator and eminent author Late Sri P.K.Srinivasan, we began as a humble neighbourhood library in Chennai, with about 5000 books. As a policy, on an average for every new member who joined, we added 20 books. Today we have 1000+ members and 25000+ books!

OTHER LIBRARY SERVICES

Patrons walk into our K.K.Nagar library to borrow books and spend time reading in the calm spelt by the embalmed minds that surround them. We also have a mobile van-library service (inaugurated in 2006 by the DD Director Sri Tejinder Singh Gagan) that goes places. Read what The Hindu said about our mobile library here!
