Online Library

stories (bookworms’ library) has been trying its best to meet the voluminous demand of booklovers through our static library at KK Nagar since 2001. The facilities we offer are very affordable and many have voiced on their sincere appreciation. Although there are a few members who take the pain of traveling over 10kms from their homes to our KK Nagar library , we at bookworms, felt the need to cater to many others, who are Internet users, who do not have a good library around their place can enjoy and fire their reading habits to sky limits. And that’s how “bookworms’ library online” is now on.
