Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Author : JK Rowling
Reviewed by : A Abishek (12)
Genre classification by reviewer : Magic and Fantasy
Bottomline : Fighting evil wizards and restoring peace
Age group : 10 +


  Harry Potter along with his best friends, Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, starts his third year at Hogwarts' school of witchcraft and wizardry. News reaches that there is a mass murderer on the loose and that he has escaped from Azkaban, a prison tightly gaurded by dementors. The name of the mass murderer was Sirius Black. Harry faints several times due to the dementors. Once, while playing a Quidditch match, the dementors come to the stadium. This makes Harry faint and Griffindor loses the match. Prof.Lupin turns into a werewolf on full-moon day and these leads to a series of events. Harry was also surprised to receive a "Firebolt" broomstick as a gift from Black. It turns out to be that Sirius is innocent and he is actually Harry's god-father. Prof.Trelawney tells Harry that Lord Voldemort and his servants are going to rise again. Will they rise really? Will Sirius be proved innocent? Who is the real murderer? Read the book to find out...
