Heidi

Author : Johanna Spyri
Reviewed by : Meghana Narayanan (8)
Genre classification by reviewer : Children Fiction
Bottomline : The fruits of true friendship
Age group : 5 +


  This is a story bout a small and a beautiful girl called Heidi who lives with her aunt in the snow-capped mountains of Switzerland. Young readers will like this story because of Heidi's kindness towards her invalid friend, Clara. Heidi shows true friendship and nurses Clara back to health. Clara becomes well again. This story develops our minds and makes us understand the importance of friendship.


A Review by Meghana Narayanan
