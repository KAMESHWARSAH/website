Member Recommends

(1) Our Iceberg is Melting - John Kotter

The story goes in such a way that you never want to postpone the reading. It teaches anyone who is in a business or the business of life, the Chief or just student, as how to change to situations and suceed under any conditon.

Recommended by Ashok Kumar

(1) Pakistan's Islamic Bomb - Maj Gen D K Palit

This book explains the genesis of Pakistan's Secret nuclear programme- how Bhutto began as early as 1965 to consider converting it to a weapons programme and how he gradually abandoned his pro-Western policy in favour of a pro-Arab one to establish an Arab connectionwith a conceptual West Asian Islamic bloc.

Recommended by Seshasayee Gopi

(2) Ekki Dokki - General Folk Tale

Its a beautifully coloured book which makes you pick at sight, told by famous writer. Two sisters are living in a town one with one hair and other with two hair. So are their names. This story narrates how caring and humane one should live with. The pictures of ekki & dokki inspires you to draw. We enjoyed it. Just go indulge.....


Recommended by Anjana Jaishankar
