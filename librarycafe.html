Library Cafe

stories library cafe (bookworms' library), established in Dec 2001, today, caters to about 2000 members in and around KK Nagar, Chennai, with about 20000 materials of both books and magazines...

Book lovers find this library very convenient as the ambience is pleasing and the reading room is an important value addition. Children enjoy reading as they can conveniently loll on bean bags and the adults with a magazine on hand forgetting the worries of the mundane world for a while. Books and other materials are being added frequently depending upon the demand and age groups.

This stories library cafe, as a resource centre, helps in upgrading the subject knowledge among students (School and Engineering) through the collections.>

The stories library cafe at KK nagar is open from Tuesday to Sunday from morning 10’O clock to night 8’O clock. Monday, is the weekly holiday. For more information please contact us.
